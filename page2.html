These listed ingredients are banned outside of the United States.
  • Blue #1 food coloring
  • Blue # 2 food coloring
  • Yellow # 5 food coloring
  • Yellow # 6 food coloring
  • Red #40 food coloring
  • Brominated vegetable oil (found in sodas)
  • Bromated flour (used in baking)
  • Olean (found in potato chips)
  • Preservatives butylated hydroxyanisole (BHA) and butylated hydroxytoluene (BHT)
  • Growth hormones rBGH and rBST (found in dairy products)
  • Arsenic found in chicken feed
